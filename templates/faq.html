Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I convert text to handwriting?

Simply enter the text in the provided text box and click on the "Try it Now" button to convert it into handwriting.

Can I upload my own handwriting?

Yes, you can upload your handwriting image and use the Handwriting to Text feature to convert it into typed text.

Is there a limit to the amount of text I can input?

No, there is no fixed limit, but we recommend keeping your input text under 1000 characters for the best performance.

What formats are supported for handwriting uploads?

We currently support JPG, PNG, and JPEG image formats for handwriting uploads.
